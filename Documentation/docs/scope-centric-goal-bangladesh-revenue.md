# User scope and centric goal — Bangladesh revenue work

**For agents:** Use this to create relevant overviews and conduct tailored research. Stay within these boundaries.

---

## Centric goal

**Define and document revenue in Bangladesh (Dhaka-centric) where the user is the direct beneficiary:** which flows exist, how they are triggered, and the exact moment (“gesture”) when revenue is recognized. Exclude government tax. Outputs should be clear and simple (tables, diagrams, plain-language explanation).

---

## Scope boundaries

**In scope**

- Geography: Bangladesh; emphasis on operations from **Dhaka**.
- Time: **Last 6 months** (Jul–Dec 2025 / FY2025-26 H1), unless a different window is specified.
- Revenue streams where the user can be the **direct beneficiary**: remittance, export (e.g. RMG), e-commerce/digital (card, MFS), FDI, domestic business (retail, services, real estate).
- **Revenue generation cycle:** trigger → activity → moment of recognition.
- **Defining moments (“gestures”):** when we say “revenue happened” (e.g. FX credited, LC/proceeds received, invoice paid, transaction settled, deed/advance paid, FDI booked).

**Out of scope**

- **NBR / government tax** as a “revenue source that applies to me” — user is not the sole beneficiary of the state; treat as excluded from direct-beneficiary analysis.
- Revenue that cannot be attributed to a direct beneficiary (e.g. aggregate government revenue only, with no “your” angle).

---

## Key questions for overview and research

1. **Where is revenue generated?** Which flows (remittance, export, e-commerce, FDI, domestic) and at what approximate scale in the period?
2. **How is it triggered?** What starts the flow (order, send, sale, investment) and what conditions (policy, demand, channels)?
3. **When is it “revenue”?** What is the defining moment or gesture (e.g. credit in account, LC realized, payment received)?
4. **What is the cycle?** Short chain: trigger → activity → settlement/recognition for each in-scope stream.
5. **Dhaka link?** How do these flows connect to operations or beneficiaries based in Dhaka (where relevant)?

---

## Deliverable style

- **Tables** for sources, scales, and defining moments.
- **Mermaid** for revenue cycle and gesture points (keep simple).
- **Plain-language explanation** (e.g. explain like 19): concise, “your” revenue, no jargon.
